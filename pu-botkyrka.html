Transitional Atmospheres

Master's Project

  • Teamwork
  • 2019
  • Urban Design

The main idea of the project is to provide safe and comfortable transitions for human and non-human actors. It is driven from analysing the facilities in Norsborg that offer services for people with mental dissabilities and the lack of safe connections among them.
