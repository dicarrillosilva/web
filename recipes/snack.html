Snickers porridge
nutrients

iron

magnesium

ingredients

  • 50 g fine oats
  • 200 ml low-fat milk
  • 1 tbsp de-oiled cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp natural peanut butter
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 pinch unrefined salt
  • 200 g low-fat yogurt

steps

  1. In a saucepan, heat milk with a pinch of salt.
  2. Stir in oats and cocoa then leave to cook on low heat for approx. 15 minutes. Stir occasionally to form a thick puree.
  3. Remove saucepan from stove and stir in the peanut butter and banana. Mash banana with a fork while folding it into porridge.
  4. Serve porridge alongside the yogurt.
