snickers porridge

nutrients

iron

magnesium

ingredients

  • 50 g fine oats
  • 200 ml low-fat milk
  • 1 tbsp de-oiled cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp natural peanut butter
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 pinch unrefined salt
  • 200 g low-fat yogurt

steps

  1. in a saucepan, heat milk with a pinch of salt.
  2. stir in oats and cocoa then leave to cook on low heat for approx. 15 minutes. Stir occasionally to form a thick puree.
  3. remove saucepan from stove and stir in the peanut butter and banana. Mash banana with a fork while folding it into porridge.
  4. serve porridge alongside the yogurt.

crisp mozarella slices

nutrients

calcium

ingredients

  • 7 slices sliced crisp bread (whole-grain)
  • 125 g low-fat mozarella
  • 40 g alfalfa sprouts
  • unrefined salt
  • black pepper

steps

  1. cut mozzarella into slices.
  2. place mozzarella on bread slices.
  3. garnish with alfalfa sprouts and season with salt and pepper to taste.

apple and nut quark

nutrients

calcium

magnesium

protein

ingredients

  • 200 g low-fat quark
  • 30 g chopped walnuts
  • 0.5 diced apples
  • 50 ml water
  • ground cinamon

steps

  1. mix low-fat quark and water in a bowl.
  2. add the rest of the ingredients and mix.
